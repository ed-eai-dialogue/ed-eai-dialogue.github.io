    Program

    We expect around 50 attendees from academic, national labs, and private sectors participating in the workshop. The program will include 6 invited talks split into 3 sessions. There will be three poster sessions from accepted papers to encourage active interaction among participants and presenters.

    Five Student Travel Awards will be selected to help broaden participation, especially from the neural and cognitive science community. One Best Student Paper Award will be chosen by a panel of experts. One Discussion Panel will be organized in the afternoon. Following is a tentative template of the program:
